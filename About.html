About Us

Welcome to Merciado Amusement Park, where fun and adventure meet! Our park offers a wide range of exciting attractions, from thrilling roller coasters to relaxing family rides. Established in 1990, Merciado Park has been a popular destination for families, friends, and tourists seeking fun and entertainment. We pride ourselves on providing a safe and enjoyable experience for visitors of all ages. Whether you're here for the rides, the food, or the memorable moments, we look forward to making your visit unforgettable!
